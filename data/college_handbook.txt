IIT DOLAKHPUR (IITD) — STUDENT HANDBOOK 2024-25
================================================

ABOUT THE INSTITUTION
---------------------
Indian Institute of Technology Dolakhpur (IITD) was established in 1998 under the
IIT Act and is located on a 620-acre campus in Dolakhpur, Madhya Pradesh. The institute
offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programmes across 14 departments.
IITD is recognised by UGC, accredited by NAAC (A++ grade), and consistently ranked
among the top 25 engineering institutions in India by NIRF.

ACADEMIC CALENDAR
-----------------
The academic year is divided into two semesters:
  - Autumn Semester: July 25 – November 30 (End-Semester Exams: Nov 20–30)
  - Spring Semester: January 6 – May 10 (End-Semester Exams: Apr 28 – May 10)

Mid-semester examinations are held in the 8th week of each semester. Supplementary
examinations for students with backlogs are conducted in June every year.

LIBRARY
-------
The Central Library is open from 8:00 AM to 11:00 PM on all working days and
9:00 AM to 6:00 PM on Sundays and public holidays. Each student may borrow up to
6 books at a time with a loan period of 14 days. Overdue fine is ₹5 per day per book.
Digital resources including IEEE Xplore, Springer, and ACM Digital Library are
accessible on campus Wi-Fi.

CANTEEN AND MESS FACILITIES
----------------------------
The Main Canteen operates from 7:30 AM to 10:00 PM. The Night Canteen (near Hostel
Block C) is open from 10:00 PM to 1:00 AM. Mess timings are: Breakfast 7:30–9:00 AM,
Lunch 12:30–2:00 PM, Snacks 5:00–6:00 PM, Dinner 7:30–9:00 PM. Mess rebate
applications must be submitted 3 days in advance at the hostel office.

HOSTEL RULES
------------
All hostellers must return to their respective hostels by 11:00 PM on weekdays.
Late entry requires written permission from the Warden. Overnight absence must be
reported via the Leave Management Portal at least 24 hours in advance. Ragging in
any form is strictly prohibited and punishable under UGC regulations.

CODE OF CONDUCT
---------------
Students must carry their IITD Identity Card at all times on campus. Use of
personal vehicles is prohibited for first-year students. Consumption of alcohol
and tobacco on campus premises is strictly forbidden. Any act of academic
dishonesty including plagiarism will result in an F grade and disciplinary action.
